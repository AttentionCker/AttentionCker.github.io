About
Read This Only If You Want To Know About Me!
I am a Mechanical Engineer and a Robotics Enthusiast who is seeking to build a fine career in the field of Robotics (Research or Application, I’m down for it all). I have a really good grasp of mathematical tools handed to me by my teachers (So I think I can confidently say that I am “good at maths”). I really love Physics and Mathematics, especially their applications. Well equipped with multiple problem solving skills also makes me a good programmer (Almost like zero hesitation taking up any technical work/challenge). And ofcourse a developer as well.
Everything has a reason, and I desperately want to know it. What differentiates me from most is my obsession with quality of work (that’s what they say). “I like to waste time making good stuff” - me. I am highly detail oriented (basically the Nitpicker. I hope you don’t dislike people of my kind). I get the job done and also make sure it is a piece of Art. I’m 92.71 percent sure that you will love me, and my work even more.
I love video games and cartoon shows. I play football and love bicycle riding. I can sketch really well. I am really good at most things I pursue. I am a little funny here and there. You know… Just a little. I am basically an explorer like a TurtleBot juiced up with SLAM components. If I did not cover anything that you might want to know about me, feel free to get in touch.
